Cormann

Cormann  was born on 6/17/2008, he is a 17.0 Bay Holsteiner Gelding who was imported from Europe in 2017 but joined team on 9/12/2024.  Cormann used to jump the really big sticks and you can see that by the way he goes, he has a lot of power in that hind end.  But don’t let that fool you, he is the sweetest and kindest horse you can find wishing to never hurt a fly!  He still likes to jump the big sticks but the vets would prefer he stays lower lever and he is happy with that for now.

Frappachino
aka Chino

Frappachino aka Chino  was born somewhere in 2005, we think, and is a 15.2h Palomino Quarter Horse Gelding. He joined team on 11/19/2024 and has been an absolute delight.  He quickly put on some weight and muscling and is proving to be the bestest and safest boy.  He tends to greet with you with pinned back ears but that is just his hello, give him a scratch on the withers and he is a happy boy.  Chino has come so far in his trust of us and we couldn’t be happier with this addition to our team!

Alderwood
aka Dublin

Alderwood aka Dublin born on 6/9/2019 a 16.2h Bay Oldenburg Gelding who joined the team on 12/5/2022 and is owned by Susanna Janska.  Dublin is a funny quirky guy who makes funny faces but deep down is is the sweetest boy.  He tries hard and doesn’t want to do it wrong.  We have been working at developing him and can’t wait to see where these two end up in the show ring.

Mastermind
aka Charlie

Mastermind aka Charlie is a 13.2h Dunn Welsh Pony Gelding, born 05/15/2007.  Charlie is the matriarch to Tiffany & Equestrians beginning August of 2017, he is the pony who started it all.  He is also the best friend to Lennox, Tiffany’s heart horse.  Charlie has a large personality and often feels misunderstood.  He always wants to be the first one picked to ride and doesn’t understand when he isn’t.  Charlie is a fancy pants who has done almost everything a pony can do.  He is hopefully on his way to Pony Finals this year to knock that event off of his bucket list with his rider Izma Alam.

Capelo du Milon
aka Cappy

Capelo du Milon aka Cappy is 13 years old, 16.2h and we welcomed him to the team on March 19, 2024. Cappy is a stunning grey who was imported from France many years ago.  He used to jump the 1.45m but is now playing in the Hunters with his owner Vanessa Bernhoft.
